About

Entrepreneurship is beyond business. It is a way of resolving the labyrinth by multiple paths and choosing the best; it's a lifestyle. Get ready with your elevator pitches for the very first edition of 'THE SUMMER STARTUP FESTIVAL' organized by Government Engineering College Thrissur in association with Swavalamban Chair for MSME Solutions funded by Small Industries Development Bank of India (SIDBI). An arena to explore your true potential while think tanks, top investors and policymakers with diverse expertise gather and guide you, all under the arch.



Join us on 14th and 15th of May !!


Let's celebrate innovation and spark the skills in you. Get to learn from real time entrepreneurs and their incredible journeys, as we gather the best startups. A free ticket to this enlivening weekend assures you a platform to exchange knowledge and insights, and above all grow your network.
